How virtual reality can shape the medicine and healthcare industry

MAD(A) Project Homepage

Virtual reality technology has been making significant strides in many different fields, and one area where its potential is truly transformative is in the medicine and healthcare field. Virtual reality technology offers the chance to revolutionise many aspects in the medicine and healhcare field such as training, patient care, therapy, and even surgical procedures. To explore the potential of how virtual reality can shape the medicine and healthcare industry, a website will be designed to provide information and insights on this topic.

The layout of the webpage will be organised into different pages There will be a home page which will introduce the topic and link to the other pages using a navigation bar. The home page will also inlcude images centred around the theme of virtual reality and healthcare. The webiste will then branch into distinct web pages. Each of these web pages will address specific aspects of the topic. Some of these topics will inlcude medical training. patient rebahilitation and surgical simulations. Each web page will also include audio and video elements such as virtual tours of VR equipped hospitals, interviews with experts and demonstrations of VR applications in healthcare. I will style all the web pages using a css file which will include the styling of all the web pages to ensure consistency throughout.
